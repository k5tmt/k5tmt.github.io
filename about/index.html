About

Hello, my name is Matt. I’m a software technician in Leander, TX. Some of my interests and hobbies include: geocaching, amateur radio, classic car restoration, gardening, home improvement, computer systems administration, cooking, and NASCAR.

What is this website’s purpose? This site will mainly focus on amateur radio, with other stuff thrown into the mix from time to time. I’m not a huge proponent of blogs and oftentimes have difficulty transferring an idea to paper. I cannot promise much content besides a few sentences here and there, but I’ll do what I can. Thanks for stopping by!
