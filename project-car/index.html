Project Car

This is my 1976 Toyota Celica GT. It has a 20R/22R hybrid engine, W58 5-speed manual transmission, dual Dellorto DHLA 40 carburetors with K&N filters, and an MSD ignition. The project started in 2013 and took about three months to piece together. I haven’t had as much time lately to work on it, but hopefully that will be changing soon. She may not be the fastest ride around, but it’s a blast to drive!

I’m hoping to start working on the car again in Fall 2019.
